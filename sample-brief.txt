PROJECT BRIEF: SBI YFI Social Media Campaign Q2 2026

Client: SBI YFI
Campaign Title: "Future Forward" Digital Launch
Deadline: March 15, 2026

OVERVIEW:
SBI YFI is launching a new digital banking feature targeting millennials and Gen Z users. The campaign needs to build awareness and drive app downloads through a multi-platform social media push across Instagram, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn.

OBJECTIVES:
- Increase app downloads by 30% within Q2
- Generate 500K impressions across all platforms in the first month
- Build brand awareness among 18-35 age demographic
- Create viral-ready content for organic sharing

DELIVERABLES:
1. 12 Instagram carousel posts (3 per week for 4 weeks)
2. 8 Instagram Reels / short-form videos (15-30 seconds each)
3. 20 Twitter/X posts with custom graphics
4. 4 LinkedIn articles with thought leadership content
5. 1 hero video (60 seconds) for campaign launch
6. Social media content calendar for the full quarter
7. Monthly analytics report template

BRAND GUIDELINES:
- Primary colors: Deep blue (#003366), Gold accent (#FFB800)
- Tone: Professional yet approachable, tech-forward, trustworthy
- Target audience: Young professionals, 22-35, urban India
- Avoid: Overly casual language, memes without context, competitor mentions

BUDGET: INR 8,00,000 (Eight Lakhs)
